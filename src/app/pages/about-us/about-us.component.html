Bob's Story
Founded in 1989
To best understand our family and where we began is a letter from my father, Peter to his father, Bob. This is how our story begins:

Dad,

I don't feel I was particularly honest as a child. I did get in trouble when I was younger more often then I would like to admit but that eventually allowed me to join you in your favorite hobby. I know you brought me along so I would stay out of trouble but watching you work on electronics really inspired me, even though I pretended I didn’t care. Over those years I grow to enjoy building, breaking, and tuckering with all sorts of electrons with you. Those really were some of best years of my life, so far.

As you know, I have been out of school long enough to know what I want to do with my life. Now that I have grow and experienced some of the world, I am writing you this letter to ask an important question. I want to ask you if you would join me by opening a computer repair store with me. Before you answer, I do have a small condition. I would like to name the shop after you. You have inspired me and encouraged me for as long as I can remember. Now I would like to make you proud (and mom, of course).

Hopefully this letter with arrive before I do but I know you will be proud of me regardless of what you decide. I can’t wait to see you and mom and have some of delicious apple pie.

Your son,

Peter
